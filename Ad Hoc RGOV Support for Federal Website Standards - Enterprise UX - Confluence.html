Ad Hoc RGOV Support for Federal Website Standards

  • Created by Bersofsky, Yvonne M. (Contractor), last modified on Jan 22, 2025

HTML Page Titles & Meta Page Descriptions

Best Practices

Content

  • Use a descriptive, unique, and concise HTML page title for every page of your website.

  • The description should be unique and include key information about the page content.
  • Include a clear call to action (CTA).
Character Count
  • The title must be at least 50 characters and no longer than 60 characters.
    • Anything longer than 60 may be truncated in search results.
    • Anything shorter than 50 characters will not provide enough detail to users.
    • Count everything between <title> and </title>, including spaces.
  • The description must be at least 50 characters and no longer than 160 characters.
    • It should include enough detail for users to understand what information or resources the page provides. 
    • Count everything inside the content quotation marks, including spaces.
Format
  • Home page: <title>Site Name - Tagline</title>
  • All other pages: <title>Page Title - Site Name</title>
<meta name="description" content="Call to action and description" /> 

Additional Resources:

  • https://standards.digital.gov/standards/meta-page-description
  • https://standards.digital.gov/standards/html-page-title/
