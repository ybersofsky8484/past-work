    • As a program staff member, I want to be able to identify all users who have a specific area of expertise, so I can match reviewers with applicable proposals and panels.

      Scope:

      • This user story is to update free text ("other") areas of expertise in parsdb.dbo.nsf_user_aoe that are duplicates of existing options in parsdb.dbo.nsf_user_aoe_lkup.

      Dependencies:

      • This task has dependencies on https://jira.nsf.gov/browse/ACCT-23832 because some of the proposed replacement AOEs will not be available until ACCT-23832 has been completed.

      Acceptance Criteria:

      • For each row in users-to-convert-040425.xlsx, update the record in parsdb.dbo.nsf_user_aoe where NSF_ID and oth_aoe_txt in the spreadsheet match NSF_ID and oth_aoe_txt in the db.
      • Make the following changes for each record:
        • db.oth_aoe_txt = ''
        • db.maj_code = xlsx.maj_code
        • db.spec_code = xlsx.spec_code
      • Example: update_user_aoes.sql
